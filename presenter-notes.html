Self-Asked Harsh Questions — Prepare These

“Why should anyone trust AI-generated exam questions?”

“How do you prevent inaccuracies?”
Curriculum grounding through RAG retrieval. But we haven’t formally measured accuracy yet — that’s an open validation need.
“What if the photo scoring is wrong?”
Every score includes a confidence rating. Below 0.4 confidence, we flag for human review. For production high-stakes exams, we recommend expert oversight.

  • Use the word “hallucination” — say “accuracy boundary” or “confidence limit”
